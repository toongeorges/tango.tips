Looking Ahead

First, I wish you all a Happy 2018!

Our first milonga of the year will take place in the Big Box hall of the Eskimofabriek.

We organise together with milonga Recuerdos Amorosos a New Year's Tango Day in Ghent! From 11h30 until 16h30 you can go to the brunch milonga La Yumba (we do not provide food, you can bring your brunch), From 17h until 22h you can go to the milonga Recuerdos Amorosos.

You find pictures of and information about the dance floor at the link http://events.eskimofabriek.be/zalen/small-box-big-box.aspx. Above, you see the dance floor empty. We have chairs, tables, audio equipment and lights to decorate the room, and we will provide beverages, so do not worry, the floor will look nice. We are organising a milonga in an empty hall, because eventually we would like to organise a milonga in the banquet hall of the ICC. We will not organise a milonga in the ICC this year, but if all goes well, we will decide around summer if we will organise a big tango event in the ICC in 2019. The milonga in the Eskimofabriek is a preparation for a possible big tango event in Ghent later on.
